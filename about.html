Exceptional
Journeys

Set off on remarkable, deeply personal trips planned by award-winning specialists

The richer the experience,
the greater the reward
As a luxury travel company, we design one-of-a-kind journeys for those who appreciate luxury in all its forms. For us, life’s greatest pleasures come from exceptional experiences. Waking up at an exclusive tented camp, surrounded by the sights, sounds and wildlife of Africa’s richest delta. Touring some of Europe’s most famous sights after-hours without another soul in sight. Setting off on a hike through the Patagonian wilderness, an incredible guide by your side. Or simply relaxing on the beach in Southeast Asia with a private dinner under the stars. The trips we craft span seven continents. We take you on journeys that get under the skin of the place you’re visiting, introducing you to local cultures and customs – as well as providing meaningful time to connect with your loved ones or reconnect with yourself. Travelling with Jacada is as much about the people you meet as it is about the remarkable places you go and stay.
Personalised design Every journey we plan is one of a kind. We’ll plan around your personal interests, tastes and preferences, providing honest advice based on first-hand knowledge.
Seamless organisation We take care of all the details so you can enjoy the adventure. No matter where you go, you can rest assured that we’re looking after you every step of the way.
Remarkable experiences Our passionate team hand-pick the best experiences and hotels as well as expert guides who bring your destination to life with care and passion.
Positive impact When you travel with Jacada, you travel with the confidence of knowing that your trip is benefiting both the people and the places that you visit.
